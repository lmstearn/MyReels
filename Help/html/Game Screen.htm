The Game Screen features:

  • At top the Game Title Area.
  • The Background Area.
  • Thumbnail proportioned Pictures displayed in order of Rank with their Prize Amount and Picture Multiplier Labels.
  • The Random Jackpot Cumulative Total.
  • The Money Back Status Indicator and Pot.
  • The 5 Reels, their Picture Arrangements and 2 Payline indicators for each of 3 Paylines.
  • The Active Candy Spin and Bet Multiplier Buttons.
  • The Substituter / Naturals Bonus Multiplier indicator and the Free Game / Free Spin Feature Multiplier indicator.
  • The Current Bet Indicator, Current Win Indicator and the Cumulative Money On Hand Indicator.
  • The Current Win Indicator has a Tooltip that has, excepting Feature Multipliers, a breakdown of the Prize Amounts paid on each active Payline.
  • The Game Messages Area.
  • The Quotes Area.
Notes:
The appearance and/or visibility of the above items are set through the Profile Configuration Manager Screen.
As all keys are disabled during Features, the recommended way then to exit the form via Quit, Change Directory, or Configure, is to right click on the Background, wait until the pop-up menu appears and select the required command.
Use the popup menu or press "Enter" for the Profile Configuration Manager Screen or press "A" for the About Screen.
Click on one of the icon sized Pictures to access the Picture Info screen.
