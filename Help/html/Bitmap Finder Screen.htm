The Bitmap Finder Screen

This is setup solely for use in conjunction with the Quote Thumbnails in the Generic Profile Configuration and comprises of:

  • A 7 X 10 array of values (indices) and names,
  • A "Previous" button when activated show a similar table to the above for lower values (indices). Stepping through until the lowest index shows as 1 the button is renamed "Back To Quote Thumbnails."
Notes:
The Bitmap Finder Screen enables a faster location of Quote Thumbnails on the Configure Thumbnails Screen. The Finder is loaded on a right- click of any blue or orange Quote Thumbnails indices.
Left-click on any label to quit the Bitmap Finder and re-activate the Configure Thumbnails Screen, where the desired Picture is now displayed on the top of the RHS List.
Right-click on any label for text output of the quotes assigned to its Picture.
To sort the list, left-click on any part of the white background where the Tooltip "click to sort" is visible. Click the same area again to "unsort" into the original chronological context. The last items in the chronologically/alphanumeric sorted list are always displayed first.
To cancel, hit Escape, or continue clicking the "Previous button".
