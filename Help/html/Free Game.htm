Free Game

A Free Game Feature occurs when the Reels are automatically spun a specified number of times at no extra cost.The trigger is the appearance of Pictures that have been configured through the Free Games Tab located in the Picture Properties Screen.

Other factors determining the behaviour of Free Games are Free Game & Free Spin Contentions, whether the Free Game trigger combination wins a Prize Amount, how many Free Games are allocated and whether the Free Games are Stored or used straight away in a Feature-Reset. There are also win Feature Multipliers and No Win Bonuses for Free Games.

Note on nomenclature: A Free Game is equivalent to a Free Spin with no Reels held during the Feature.
