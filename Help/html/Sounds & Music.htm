Sounds & Music

The Sounds & Music environment of this game offers Midi and native wave playback for your Audio Device. The choice of Midi and Wave hearkens back to the days of multimedia games running on DOS and early versions of Windows.

The Sounds Tab of the General Options Screen offers dialogs for new themes for which the internet is an abundant source of files.

Editing of the midi files for versions prior to 3.0 was with an early version of Musicator, editing of the default set for 3.X through Reaper.

Note for Windows versions
