Pot

This term refers to the current Random Jackpot value and the amount of cash stored next to the POT label in the Money Back area, both displayed on the Game Screen.

If the Random Jackpot is won, the Pot reverts to its initial setting as specified in the Profile Configuration Manager Screen. The Money Back Pot always reverts to zero after it is won.

Notes:
Pots are automatically reset to their starting amounts if either, or both the Random Jackpot and Money Back Features are disabled during the game.
Upon quitting the game, the Pot is always preserved for the next play.
