Lit

Picture Multiplier Labels and Prize Amount labels and appropriate Payline indicators on the Game Screen are Lit when the Picture appears in a winning combination. The two middle Payline indicators also light up when a Scatter Prize Amount is won. The colour and mode of the light can be changed in the Appearance Tab of the General Options Screen.

During a Random Jackpot payout, prizelabels of pending Prize Amounts will be Lit, but not indicated on the prizemeter until the Random Jackpot Prize Amount has been totalled.
