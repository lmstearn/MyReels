Cheat

A 'cheat' is effected by pressing 'C' or selecting the cheat option in the pop-up menu on the Game Screen. Prize Amounts and Statistics from a 'cheat' Spin aren't retained and Features won't be triggered. Successive cheat Spins will cycle the prizes through to the highest Prize Amounts of best Ranking Pictures on the middle Payline. In a Multiline game, top and bottom Payline winning combinations aren't paid either.
