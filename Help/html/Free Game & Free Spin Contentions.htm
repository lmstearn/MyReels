Free Game & Free Spin Contentions

If there are two or more Pictures each with Free Game or Free Spin options selected, the situation of contention arises. For example, suppose Picture A is a Scatter and allows for a Free Spin on Reels 1 & 2 and Picture B a Free Game on Reels 3, 4 & 5. If this combination actually turns up after a Spin the decision to start either Feature A or Feature B first is determined by the relative position of Pictures A and B on the upper right section of the Profile Configuration Manager Screen.

Similar treatment applies to the restoring of saved or Stored Features A & B. At the completion of the current Feature, A will be restored before B if it lies above B in the upper right section of the Profile Configuration Manager Screen.

In a Multiline game, the middle Payline is always considered first, the bottom Payline, when active, is always considered last. For example, Feature combinations with no Feature-Reset attribute appearing on the middle Payline, will always be Stored regardless of order, whenever a Feature-Reset occurs on the top or bottom Paylines.
