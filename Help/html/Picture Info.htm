The Picture Info Screen comprises of:

Five text items containing information on Picture Properties of the selected Picture:

  1. Indicates the Pay Method.
  2. Indicates the Picture if a Substituter, and what it substitutes for.
  3. Indicates the Reels the Substituter is active on.
  4. Indicates whether there is a Free Spin or Free Game, the quantity available, whether it's Feature-Resettable or accumulates as a Stored item.
  5. Indicates what Reels the Feature operates on.
Notes:
Press Space, Esc or click anywhere on the Picture Info screen to return to the Game Screen.
An installed Courier New font is required to view this screen.
