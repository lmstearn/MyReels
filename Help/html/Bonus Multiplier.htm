Bonus Multiplier

The Substituter and Naturals Bonus Multiplier is applied only when there is a pair or more of a Picture in the above two Prize Categories contributing to its Prize Amount. Single Prize Level wins, alone or in combination, don't get the Bonus Multiplier.

The Naturals Bonus Multiplier operates when at least one of the Pictures in the paying combination is a Naturals and belongs to a Reel that is active (or ticked) on the General Tab in the Picture Properties Screen.

The Substituter Bonus Multiplier operates when at least one of the Pictures used in the paying combination is a Substituter.
