Optimal Money Back Strategy

The Optimal Money Back Strategy offers the best betting plan for minimising loss. If the Value Of Game is greater than 100% then no Strategy is required because we will never expect to make a loss. Strategies can be viewed after selecting the Generate button on the About Screen. A typical Optimal Strategy might be 1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 1, 5, 5, 5. That suggests that we bet 1 while the Money Back Status Indicator on the Game Screen does not exceed 7 and bet 5 otherwise.

A non zero Probability Of No Prizes is required to determine such a Strategy and is based on single Payline probabilities only. Formulation of Optimal Money Back Strategies in a Multiline game will be put on ice for a future Version.
