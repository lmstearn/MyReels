Welcome to MyReels Version 3.2!

This is a Freeware game compiled in VB6 intending to promote that little personal touch we all find so lacking in todays greedy and flashy money machines. You'll find a comprehensive help on the current Game Version with Contents and Search available on any of the Screens when the F1 key is pressed.

For your convenience the Help file has two types of links: internal and external. Issues can arise displaying external pages in HTMLHelp, and some of their content may not be visible unless this HTMLHelp window is enlarged or maximised.

Playing the game is straightforward and facilitated by keystrokes; obtained from a pop-up meny by pressing the shift key or clicking the right mouse button on the Game Screen Background.

Note that the recommended Screen Resolution is 1024 X 768 or 1366 X 768 for widescreen. For other info, please refer to Installation & Issues and ...

Good luck and enjoy!
