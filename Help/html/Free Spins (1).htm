The Free Spins (1) Tab of the Picture Properties Screen features:

  • A choice of patterns to trigger the Feature. For a description of patterns, see Pay Method. Patterns with the highest number of Pictures are always given precedence i.e. fours then triples then pairs.
Note:
The given choices of Free Spins in the Tab range over Reels containing at least one of the selected Picture, regardless of the number and distribution of possible Substituters. If a Substituter is used, this restricted choice doesn't prevent Feature triggers on Reels where our Substituted Picture doesn't exist.
