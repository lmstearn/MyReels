No Win Bonus

In a Free Game / Free Spin Feature the Player can select the No Win Bonus option. This happens during a Feature when a Prize Amount is not won on the Payline having the initial Feature combination, and adds to the Feature win total, the value of the bet at the beginning of the Feature, multiplied by a factor configured in the Free Games or Free Spins (2) Screens.

The Money Back Feature is another type of No Win Bonus scheme.

Note: No Win Bonuses aren't nullified on any Random Jackpot wins during the Feature .
