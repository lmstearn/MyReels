Free Spin

A Free Spin Feature occurs when a specified number of Reels are held and the remaining Reels are automatically spun a specified number of times, at no extra cost.The trigger is the appearance of Pictures that have been configured through the Free Spins (1) and Free Spins (2) Tabs located in the Picture Properties Screen.

Other factors determining the behaviour of Free Spins are Free Game & Free Spin Contentions, whether the Free Spin trigger combination wins a Prize Amount, how many Free Spins are allocated and whether the Free Spins are Stored or used straight away in a Feature-Reset. There are also win Feature Multipliers and No Win Bonuses for Free Spins.

Pictures on held Reels are never used for, or Stored in, trigger combinations for a new Feature.
