Ordinatio Partialis

A Reel with an Ordinatio Partialis Picture means it does not have that Picture configured in the Edit Reels Configuration Screen.

The following tabulates the possible combinations together with their rather subjectively associated Pay Methods:

Single Picture only: Any.
Pair together on left: Left To Right only.
Pair together on right: Right To Left only.
Pair in combinations other than the two above: Any.
Triplet together on left: Left To Right only.
Triplet together on right: Right To Left only.
Triplet together in middle (Reels 2, 3, 4): Middle Threes only.
Triplet in combinations other than the three above: Any.
Quadruplet together on left: Left To Right & Middle Threes only.
Quadruplet together on right: Right To Left & Middle Threes only.
Quadruplet in combinations other than the two above: Any.
