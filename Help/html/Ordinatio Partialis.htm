Ordinatio Partialis

It's possible for a Reel to have no Picture configured (zero value) in the Edit Reels Configuration Screen. The ensuing Ordinatio Partialis arrangements can vary from just one to four of the remaining Reels having the Picture.

The following tabulates all possible Ordinatio Partialis combinations together with their rather subjectively associated Pay Methods:

Single Picture only: Any.
Pair together on left: Any, Left To Right.
Pair together on right: Any, Right To Left.
Pair in combinations other than the above two: Any.
Triplet together on left: Any, Left To Right.
Triplet together on right: Any, Right To Left.
Triplet together in middle (Reels 2, 3, 4): Any, Middle Threes.
Triplet in combinations other than the above three: Any.
Quadruplet together on left: Any, Left To Right & Middle Threes.
Quadruplet together on right: Any, Right To Left & Middle Threes.
Quadruplet on Reels 1,2,4,5: Any, Left To Right & Right To Left.
Quadruplet in combinations other than the above three: Any.
