Estimate

Previously it was indicated that 10 million Monte Carlo iterations would take more than 30 hours to do on a typical Win98 machine. Then came an 350Mhz Aptiva where it took almost a minute to perform 150000 iterations on maximum configuration. In 2017, a venerable AMD FX(tm)-6200 takes around 10 seconds to perform 500,000 iterations. On older machines it would take more than 5 hours for 50 million iterations, now less than 5 minutes!
