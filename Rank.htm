Rank

The Rank of a Picture is its order of display (left/top - down then right/top - down) in the various Profile Configuration Screens and the Configure New Pictures Screen, which is different to the order of the iconised Pictures shown on the Game Screen (top/left - top/right to bottom/left - bottom/right or simply top - down if Big Pictures are used). Generally speaking, the highest Ranking Pictures are the rarest and highest priced.

Excluding Scatters, the Prize Amount for a specified Prize Level never exceeds the Prize Amount for the corresponding Prize Level of higher Ranking Pictures.

Rank cannot be changed in any current Profile Configuration.
