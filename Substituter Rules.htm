Substituter Rules

A Picture can be a Substituter if it satisfies the following criteria:

  • Not a Scatter.
  • Not another Substituter.
  • The Substituted is not used by another Substituter
  • Feature triggers can be used after all Prize Amounts have been allocated and maximised for substitution.
  • Any non-zero Prize Amount is greater than or equal to the Prize Level of the Picture it is substituting but does not exceed those of the next Prize Level up.
  • A Substituter paying Left To Right does not substitute a Picture paying Any.
  • A Substituter paying Left To Right only, does not substitute for either a Picture paying Right To Left or Middle Threes.
  • A Substituter paying Left To Right and Middle Threes only, does not substitute for either a Picture paying Left To Right only, or Right To Left
  • A Substituter paying Left To Right and Right To Left only, does not substitute for either a Picture paying Left To Right only, or Middle Threes.
  • A Substituter can only be used once in the calculation of any Prize Amount
  • No more than 4 different Substituters are allowed.
