Prize Amount

Prize Amounts are configurable in the Picture Properties Screen, ranging from 0 to 5000. This range is subsequently affected by Bet Multiplier, Rank, Scatter and Substituter status. A Prize is paid when Pictures conform to a Pay Method of a particular Prize Level.

There are limits on Prize Levels. The following table illustrates the limits applying to whether a Picture is configured as a non Scatter or whether it has been allocated 1 or more Scatter in the Edit Reels Configuration Screen.
